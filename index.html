Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Find answers to the most common questions about our mentorship program, process, and how we can help you achieve your academic goals.

How does the Scoreazy mentorship program work? +

Our mentorship program pairs you with an experienced academic counselor who understands your unique needs. You'll have regular one-on-one sessions where you'll work together to set goals, develop study strategies, track progress, and address any academic challenges. Sessions can be conducted online or in-person based on your preference.

What qualifications do your mentors have? +

All our mentors are highly qualified professionals with advanced degrees in education, psychology, or their respective subject areas. They have extensive experience in academic counseling, college admissions, and student success coaching. Many of our mentors have worked at top universities and educational institutions.

How often will I meet with my mentor? +

Session frequency depends on your needs and chosen package. Typically, students meet with their mentors weekly or bi-weekly for 60-minute sessions. During exam periods or college application seasons, you can schedule additional sessions as needed. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your academic calendar.

Can you help with college applications and scholarships? +

Absolutely! Our mentors are experts in college admissions and scholarship applications. They'll help you identify suitable colleges, craft compelling personal statements, prepare for interviews, and navigate the entire application process. We also assist with scholarship research and application strategies.

How much does the mentorship program cost? +

We offer several packages to fit different needs and budgets. Our basic package starts at $150 per session, with discounted rates for package deals. We also offer scholarship opportunities for students who demonstrate financial need. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific requirements.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help! Schedule a free consultation to discuss your specific needs and learn more about how our mentorship program can benefit you.
